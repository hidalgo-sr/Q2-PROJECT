The Philippines is a country full of beautiful scenery, abundant natural resources, and warm people. From the mesmerizing coastlines to the inventive local crafts to the distinctive gourmet experience, you will find the kind of pleasure that is specifically yours here. Everyone is free to join in the fun. There is always something exciting to learn whether you traveling casually or investing in your own business in the Philippines Here are the top 3 destinations in the Philippines

Boracay

This is one of the country's most popular tourist destinations, and now, became an international destination too.

Puerto Princesa

This place is well-know for its marine sanctuaries, white sand beaches, and diverse animals and is ideal for outdoor enthusiats and nature lovers.

Intramuros

Intramuros is a historic walled area located in the heart of Manila, the capital city of the Philippines. It was built during the Spanish colonial period and served as the center of political, religious, and military power during that time.
